USACE Regulatory Workflow

Project Tracker for PMs

USACE Wilmington District CESAW

Track each project by entering a start date, selecting a project type, and marking the review items as Yes, No, or N/A. The dashboard auto-calculates days in review, target dates, and progress percentages. Status shows ⚠️ ⚠️ for incomplete rows, 👍 💯 for fully completed rows whether all yes or mixed, and 👎 🛑 when all responses are no or an active Add. Info Request is still outstanding.

Show typical regulatory timelines reference
PCN, Pre-Construction Notification45 day Corps review window after receipt of a complete PCN.
NWP, Nationwide PermitOften immediate if no PCN is required, 45 days when a PCN is required.
UA, Unauthorized ActionNo single definitive timeline, depends on enforcement posture, restoration needs, and submittal completeness.
IP, Individual PermitCommon tracking goal is about 120 days once the application is complete and the review process is underway.
SIP, Standard Individual PermitSimilar to IP, commonly tracked at about 120 days for workload management.
Permit Mod, Permit ModificationOften tracked around 45 days, but can vary with the scope of the requested change.
DC, Delineation ConcurrenceCommon tracking window is same day to about 30 days, depending on submittal quality and review needs.
AJD, Approved Jurisdictional DeterminationCommon tracking goal is about 60 days.
PJD, Preliminary Jurisdictional DeterminationOften same day to about 30 days if the request package is sufficient.
PREAPPCONS, Pre-Application ConsultationCommon planning and coordination target is about 30 days.
NPR, No Permit RequiredOften same day to up to 30 days depending on jurisdictional review and project information.
PGP, Programmatic General PermitCommon tracker value is about 45 days, though actual timing depends on the partner program and completeness.
RGP, Regional General PermitCommon tracker value is about 60 days, used as an expedited regional general permit review benchmark.
LOP, Letter of PermissionCommon abbreviated review track is about 60 to 90 days, this tracker uses 90 days.
